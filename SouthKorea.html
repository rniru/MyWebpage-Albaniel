South Korea

Why South Korea?

South Korea fascinates me with its perfect blend of ancient traditions and ultra-modern technology. From its vibrant K-pop culture to historic temples, from bustling street markets to serene mountain temples, South Korea offers an incredible mix of experiences. I'm drawn to its innovative technology, delicious cuisine, beautiful landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Seoul

Experience the dynamic capital city with its mix of modern skyscrapers, traditional markets, and historic palaces

Jeju Island

Explore the volcanic landscapes, beautiful beaches, and unique culture of Korea's largest island

Busan

Visit the colorful Gamcheon Culture Village, beautiful beaches, and vibrant seafood markets

Gyeongju

Discover the ancient capital filled with historic temples, royal tombs, and traditional architecture

Experiences I Look Forward To

  • Trying authentic Korean street food in Myeongdong
  • Experiencing a traditional temple stay
  • Visiting K-pop entertainment companies
  • Wearing a traditional Hanbok at Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • Relaxing in a Korean jimjilbang (traditional spa)
